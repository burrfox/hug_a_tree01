About Us


Treehugger's Forest Preservation is nonprofit organization. Our ultimate goal is the conservation of the world's forests, those that are endangered especially, and promoting awareness for enviormental issues. We partner with other conservation organizations, local groups, and communitites to coordinate and fund projects all over the world.


Our organization relies solely on donations from people like you who understand the importance of forest conservation. When you become a member you are invited to attend events and activities related to the nonprofit. We will also send you our newsletter and any updates about projects or general information with the organization.
